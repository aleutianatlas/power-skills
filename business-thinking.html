Select your level (0–10)

Compassion

Pays attention to how people are doing and responds with care. Checks in when someone seems off and shows up with kindness, not criticism.

Empathy

Tunes into how others are feeling and thinking. Pays attention to tone, body language, and subtle cues to better understand their experience.

Listening

Gives others full attention when they speak. Listens fully and reflects before responding.

Openness

Welcomes a wide range of people and perspectives. Respects differences and avoids jumping to conclusions or judging.

Respectfulness

Stays calm and kind, even under pressure. Treats people with basic decency and keeps communication professional.

Inclusion

Brings others into the process. Seeks out opinions, values feedback, and makes sure everyone feels heard before decisions are made.
