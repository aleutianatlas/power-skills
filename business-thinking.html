Welcome to the Collaboration & Influence Assessment. This survey focuses on your application of collaboration, communication, relationship building, influence, and writing skills. To get the best results, please keep these guidelines in mind:

  • Complete in one sitting. Plan for about one hour to reflect and respond thoughtfully so your progress isn’t lost.
  • Draw from your recent experience. Think about how you’ve demonstrated these skills in your most recent roles and responsibilities. Answer based on the behaviors you use most often—not what you could do if asked, but what you actually do day to day.
  • Answer honestly. There are no right or wrong answers. The goal is to capture your current use of this skill so you can gain meaningful insights into strengths and growth areas.
